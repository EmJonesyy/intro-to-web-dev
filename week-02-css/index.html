Quotes by Betty White!

Betty White is one of my most favorite actresses of all time!

She had such a positive outlook on life, and was an inspiration to so many!

I thought it would be fun to share some quotes she had said over the years.

Some quotes by Betty White are listed below!

"Everybody needs a passion. That’s what keeps life interesting. If you live without passion, you can go through life without leaving any footprints."

"Don’t try to be young. Just open your mind. Stay interested in stuff. There are so many things I won’t live long enough to find out about, but I’m still curious about them."

"Laughter keeps everyone feeling wonderful."

"If one has no sense of humor, one is in trouble."

"I know unless I am true to myself I couldn’t be happy. Too much emphasis is placed today on externals and too little on character."

I hope you enjoyed reading these quotes!
